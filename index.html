The NHS is led by four student officers – President, Vice President of Tutoring, Vice President of Services, and Secretary. NHS members are required to perform 14 hours of service each semester. This service includes tutoring at St. Benedict’s which all of my students are encouraged to take advantage of. Questions about the St. Benedict’s Chapter of the National Honor Society may be directed to its faculty advisors, James F. Duffy (jduffy@sbp.org) or Michelle Tuorto (mtuorto@sbp.org). more info

Good note-taking is based on a clear sense of why you are taking notes in the first place. Once you understand the importance of taking notes, you will be able to make your note taking much more useful - rather than jotting stuff down randomly because everyone else is too. It’s important to keep asking yourself: ‘Why am I taking notes today? What key points are useful in today's lesson? What parts or the lesson do I not understand?more info
