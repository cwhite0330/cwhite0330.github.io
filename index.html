SUMMER PHASE | MATH 9


This summer math course was designed by the freshman math department to asses the strengths and weaknesses of each incoming student at St. Benedict's. Your performance in this course will determine your course placement for the Fall Semester of 2015. Therefore, it is in your interest to put forth your very best effort.

On July 28th, all incoming students will take the initial Pre-Algebra Assessment. This assessment is a comprehensive exam of the entire St.Benedict's Pre-Algebra curriculum. Students who demonstrate proficinecy (≥70%) on this assessment will move on to take a second assessment on August 4th, 2015.

This secondary assessment will determine the course placement for the remaining freshmen. Placement for students who take the secondary assessment is limited to the following courses [Algebra 1A, Algebra 1B, or Advanced Algebra]. Students who only take the first assessment will be placed in one of the following courses [Math 1 or Pre-Algebra].

Freshman students will only earn placement in Geometry if they can demonstrate recall and mastery of the entire Freshman math curriculum. This includes all of the topics from place value to using the quadractic formula (especially, factoring quadratics and all operations involving radical expressions).
