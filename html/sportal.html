Explanation of Grade Categories:

Class Participation

Class Participation

Active engagement during class is a critical component od each student’s success. Therefore, this portion of your overall grade will be affected by your individual abilitiy to do the following :

Come to class with the appropriate course materials [pencils, class notebook, etc.]

Demonstrate the appropriate classroom behavior at all times.

  • Be respectful to your peers.
  • Do not speak out of turn.
  • Raise your hand silently and wait to be acknowledged if you have a question.
  • Complete all of your homework assignments.

All of my students will begin the semester with an A in this category of their grade. Free points right! Failure to comply with any of these expectations for your class participation will result in your grade being lowered by 10% per instance.

Ten (10) seperate class participation infractions will result in you failing this portion of your grade.

iClicker

iClicker

iClickers are simple student response devices. These tools allow me to track your mastery of each lesson, real time. Each student will be assigned an iClicker at the beginning of the semester. These devices will be handed out and recollected at the end of class periods. You should never take your assigned iClicker outside of the classroom!

During each lesson, 5—10 multiple choice questions will be presented to the class. You will have 2—4 minutes to answer each question depending on the level of difficulty. The classes collective responses for each question will be discussed along with the answers. You score for each lesson will affect your overall iClicker average.

Not responding to each question will cost you the most points; make an educated guess, even if you do not fully understand the material.

Notebook

Notebook

As I have previously outlined, your Algebra Notebook is the most important tool at your disposal this semester. You must learn to take clear and comprehensive notes for each lesson.

Just like Class Participation, all of my students will begin the semester with an A for their notebook grade. Failure to comply with my basic note taking expectations will result in in you failing this portion of your grade.

Homework

Homework

Homework for each lesson is already loaded into the S.Portal for each course. Check the due dates for each lesson and copy them into your planner.

Almost all of your homework this semester will be submitted electronically. However, any assignment submitted on looseleaf paper should have 1. The proper heading, 2. legible handwriting, and 3. work clearly showing how you came to find your final answers.

Absenses are not an excuse for missing homework that can be submitted electronically. If a homework assignment is due on looseleaf paper and you are absent the day the assignment is due, you must hand in your late homework assignment(s) on the first day you return to school.

All unexcused late/ missing homework assignments will count as a zero (0) towards your overall homework average.

Quizzes

Quizzes

There are NO POP QUIZZES in my courses. All classes will have weekly quizzes every Tuesday, covering lessons from the prior week.

Exams (3)

Exams (3)

Each of my Algebra courses will have three (3) exams throughout the semester.
